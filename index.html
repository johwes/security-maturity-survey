Machine-Speed Exploitation

Attack Path Chaining

The Governance Gap

Technical remediation speed has outpaced governance approval velocity, leaving exploitable windows that machine-speed adversaries capitalize on within minutes.

Governance Friction Factor
Gf = Tdeploy / Tapprove

When Gf ≫ 1, technical deployment capability outpaces governance approval speed — creating an open window of exposure that machine-speed adversaries exploit within minutes. Resolving this asymmetry requires pre-approved automated response policies.

01 Visibility

02 Tooling Speed

03 Approval Speed

04 Identity Gating

Mechanisms to verify users and machines before granting access to corporate assets.

05 Recovery Drills

Frequency and comprehensiveness of restoration testing to validate recovery capabilities.

Regulatory Context

NIS2 / Cybersäkerhetslagen 2025:1506

EU Network & Information Security Directive

In force in Sweden from January 15, 2026. Covers 18 critical sectors with 50+ employees or €10M+ annual turnover.

  • Incident reporting: 24h early warning → 72h detailed notification → 1-month final report
  • Board liability: CEOs and directors personally liable for cybersecurity compliance
  • Penalties: Up to €10M or 2% of global turnover for essential entities
DORA

Digital Operational Resilience Act

Mandates demonstrable operational resilience for financial sector organizations, with direct board-level accountability.

  • CTEM alignment: Continuous Threat Exposure Management satisfies NIS2 Article 21.2(e)
  • Recovery testing: Quarterly restore drills directly support DORA resilience mandates
  • Governance velocity: Pre-approved response frameworks reduce regulatory exposure
CIS Controls v8 IG1

Minimum Essential Cyber Hygiene

Implementation Group 1 defines the baseline for all organizations. Assessment action triggers map directly to IG1 controls.

  • Control 1.1: Enterprise Asset Inventory → Visibility
  • Control 6.3: Multi-Factor Authentication → Identity Gating
  • Control 7.1: Vulnerability Management → Tooling Speed
  • Control 11.1: Data Recovery Process → Recovery Drills
  • Control 17.1: Incident Management → Approval Speed
